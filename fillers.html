Overview

Our premium dermal filler treatments restore volume, smooth fine lines, and enhance your natural features. Using MHRA-approved hyaluronic acid fillers, we deliver subtle, natural-looking results that bring out your best - never overdone. Every treatment begins with a thorough consultation to ensure the approach is fully tailored to your facial structure and goals.

Treatment Process

A thorough consultation is followed by the treatment itself, which takes 30–45 minutes. A topical numbing cream is applied beforehand so discomfort is minimal. Most clients return to normal activities the same day.

Results & Longevity

  • Results visible immediately
  • Improve further over 2 weeks
  • Lasts 6–18 months
  • Fully reversible if needed

Aftercare

Avoid strenuous exercise, alcohol and direct sun exposure for 24 hours. Mild swelling or bruising may occur and typically resolves within a few days. Full aftercare instructions provided at your appointment.
