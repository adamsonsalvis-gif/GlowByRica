Overview

Anti-Wrinkle relaxes the muscles that cause expression lines, smoothing existing wrinkles and preventing new ones from forming. But it goes far beyond anti-ageing - Anti-Wrinkle is a versatile treatment used to slim, lift, and even relieve physical discomfort. Using 100% authentic, MHRA-approved product, every treatment is tailored to keep your results natural and your expressions yours.

What Can Anti-Wrinkle Treat

Forehead Lines

Smooths horizontal lines across the forehead for a relaxed, refreshed look.

Frown Lines

Reduces the "11 lines" between the brows caused by squinting or frowning.

Crow's Feet

Softens fine lines around the eyes for a brighter, more youthful appearance.

Brow Lift

Subtly lifts the brows for a more refreshed and awake look.

Bunny Lines

Smooths the diagonal lines that appear on the nose when smiling or scrunching.

Nose Tip Lift

Subtly lifts and refines the tip of the nose for a more balanced profile.

Smoker's Lines

Softens the fine vertical lines around the upper lip for a smoother appearance.

Lip Flip

Relaxes the upper lip to create a subtle, natural-looking flip for a fuller appearance.

Gummy Smile

Reduces the amount of gum shown when smiling for a more balanced, confident smile.

Downward Smile

Relaxes the muscles that pull the corners of the mouth down for a more uplifted smile.

Jawline Slimming

Slims the jawline by relaxing the masseter muscles.

Chin Dimpling

Smooths the texture of the chin for a more even appearance.

Nostril Slimming

Refines the appearance of wider nostrils.

Excessive Sweating

Reduces underarm sweat and boosts confidence.

Treatment Process

After a brief consultation, small precise injections are made into the targeted muscles. No downtime - most clients return to daily activities immediately.

Results

  • Visible within 3-14 days
  • Full results at 2 weeks
  • Lasts 3-4 months
  • Longer with regular treatments

Aftercare

Avoid lying down, heavy exercise, or touching treated areas for 4 hours. Avoid saunas and alcohol for 24 hours.
